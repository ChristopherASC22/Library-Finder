Library Cards!

A common barrier to accessing libraries are obtaining Public Library Card. There's good news tho! You can obtain a Library for free!

For people who are 13 or Up:

If you live in the New York State, the New York Public Library (NYPL) offer a free Digital card. You can get access to an array of literature! Some of these resources include E-Books.

For Children who are 12 or younger

For children younger than 12, they can apply for a different type of Library Card with their parent's or guardians. Children 12 and Under will have access to NYPL's Digital "SimplyE" service.

For more information, check out their website at https://www.nypl.org/library-card
